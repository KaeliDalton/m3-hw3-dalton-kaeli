Jamie Bower

About

Jamie Bower, born James Metcalfe Campbell Bower, is a British musician, actor, and model. He was born November 22, 1988 in London, England. He began his acting and music careers while still in school. He is currently releasing music as a solo artist under his name after his previous band dissolved in the fall of 2020. His most recent acting role is in the newest season of Stranger Things.
